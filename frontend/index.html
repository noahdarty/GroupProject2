About MindFit

Student Wellness Platform

MindFit is a wellness platform designed to help students build stronger minds and bodies through personalized workouts.

We believe that mental and physical health are deeply connected — when you take care of one, you improve the other.

Our system allows students to log their mood each day and receive a science-backed workout plan tailored to how they feel — whether they're stressed, calm, tired, or full of energy.

Each student is also paired with a personal coach who can view progress, track moods, and provide motivation based on real data.

Our Mission

"Empower every student to move better, feel better, and live better — one mood at a time."
